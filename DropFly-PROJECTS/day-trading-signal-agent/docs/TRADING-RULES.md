## The 10 Commandments of Day Trading with This System

### 1. Trade Only HIGH-Quality Signals (Most Important)

**Rule:**
- Only enter trades rated **HIGH** by the AI
- Occasionally take strong **MEDIUM** signals (after 2+ weeks of experience)
- **NEVER** trade LOW-quality signals

**Why:**
- HIGH signals have 65-75% win rate
- MEDIUM signals have 50-60% win rate
- LOW signals have <50% win rate

**Exception:**
- After 1 month of tracking, if you find certain MEDIUM setups work well for you, you may add them selectively

### 2. Maximum 2-3 Trades Per Day

**Rule:**
- No more than 3 trades in a single day
- If you hit 2 losses, stop for the day
- If you hit $300 profit, consider stopping (optional)

**Why:**
- Overtrading kills accounts
- Mental fatigue leads to mistakes
- Quality over quantity

**Example Day:**

### 4. Hard Stop Loss: -7%

**Rule:**
- Set stop loss IMMEDIATELY when entering trade
- Stop loss = -7% maximum
- **NO EXCEPTIONS**
- If hit, exit immediately

**Stop Loss Placement:**

**For LONG/CALL trades:**

- Below recent support
- Maximum: -7% from entry

**For PUT/SHORT trades:**
- Above VWAP (typically 1-2%)
- Above recent resistance
- Maximum: -7% from entry

**Example:**
- Entry: $1,000 position
- Stop loss at -7%: Exit at -$70 loss
- Actual stop should be tighter (at VWAP), usually -2-3%

**Mental Stop vs. Hard Stop:**

## Risk Management Formula

### Position Sizing Formula

```
Position Size = Account Size Ã— Risk Percentage / Stop Loss %

Example:
- Account: $10,000
- Risk per trade: 1% = $100
- Stop loss: -7%
- Position size: $10,000 Ã— 1% / 7% = $1,428

Use $1,400 (within $800-$1,500 range)
```

### Kelly Criterion (Advanced)

Once you have 50+ trades tracked:

```
Kelly % = (Win Rate Ã— Avg Win) - (Loss Rate Ã— Avg Loss) / Avg Win

Example:
- Win rate: 65%
- Avg win: +12%
- Loss rate: 35%
- Avg loss: -4%

Recommended: Use 1/4 Kelly = 1.1% per trade
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Overtrading
**Mistake:** Taking 5-10 trades per day
**Fix:** Stick to 2-3 max

### 2. Chasing Signals
**Mistake:** Entering too late, FOMO
**Fix:** If you miss entry, skip it. Wait for next signal.

### 3. Moving Stop Loss
**Mistake:** "Just a little more room..."
**Fix:** Stop is stop. No exceptions.

### 4. Not Taking Profits
**Mistake:** "+10% isn't enough, I want +20%"
**Fix:** Take the +10-15%. Be happy. Move on.

### 5. Trading MEDIUM/LOW Signals
**Mistake:** "This MEDIUM looks pretty good..."
**Fix:** Only HIGH. Period. (first month)

### 6. Ignoring AI Evaluation
**Mistake:** "I know better than the AI"
**Fix:** The AI sees more than you. Trust the evaluation.

### 7. Trading While Emotional
**Mistake:** Trading angry, stressed, or desperate
**Fix:** If emotional, stop. Trading requires clear mind.

### 8. Not Using Stops
**Mistake:** "I'll just watch it..."
**Fix:** Set stop IMMEDIATELY. No excuses.

### 9. Revenge Trading After Loss
**Mistake:** Doubling position size to recover
**Fix:** Stick to position sizing rules. Always.

### 10. Forgetting It's Probability
**Mistake:** Getting upset at a losing trade
**Fix:** 65% win rate = 35% losing trades are NORMAL
