# Customer Onboarding Checklist Template

## What This Enables

This template provides a standardized, milestone-driven onboarding checklist that ensures every new customer progresses through a structured journey from contract signing to full value realization. The checklist prevents onboarding drift (where accounts stall without anyone noticing), ensures consistent quality across CSMs, and provides clear visibility into onboarding pipeline health.

### Escalation Triggers

| Trigger | Action | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| No kickoff within 7 days of contract | CSM reaches out with urgency, CC sales rep | CSM |
| Technical setup stalled for 5+ business days | Escalate to CS leader, involve engineering if technical blocker | CSM |
| No first value action by Day 21 | Intervention call with champion, offer concierge help | CSM |
| < 30% user activation by Day 30 | Executive outreach to sponsor, enablement push | CSM + CS Leader |
| Customer unresponsive for 10+ business days | Multi-channel outreach (email, phone, champion, sponsor) | CSM + CS Leader |
| Onboarding not complete by target date | Root cause analysis, revised timeline, leadership review | CS Leader |

## Usage Notes

- Adapt timeline targets to segment: SMB (30 days), Mid-Market (60 days), Enterprise (90 days)
- The checklist is a minimum standard -- add customer-specific items as needed
- Update the CRM after each phase completion to maintain pipeline visibility
- If a customer skips or refuses a step, document the decision and adjust the plan
- The escalation triggers are not optional -- they must be acted on within the SLA
- Run a quarterly review of aggregate onboarding metrics to identify systemic improvements
