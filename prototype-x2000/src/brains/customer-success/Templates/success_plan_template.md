# Customer Success Plan Template

## What This Enables

This template provides a standardized structure for creating a Customer Success Plan -- a living document that aligns the customer and CSM on desired outcomes, milestones, responsibilities, and timelines. The success plan is the strategic backbone of the customer relationship, ensuring that every interaction is oriented toward the customer's stated business objectives.

## Usage Notes

- Create the success plan during onboarding and update at every QBR
- Share relevant sections with the customer (they should know the plan exists and have input)
- Keep the action item tracker current -- stale action items erode trust
- Review the stakeholder map at every QBR to catch relationship changes early
- This is a living document, not a one-time artifact. Plans that are not updated are worse than no plan (they create false confidence)
