# Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Template

## What This Enables

This template provides a standardized structure for conducting Quarterly Business Reviews that deliver strategic value to customers while generating insights for the CS team. A well-executed QBR strengthens the executive relationship, validates value delivery, surfaces expansion opportunities, and identifies emerging risks. The QBR is the single highest-leverage touchpoint in the high-touch CS model.

## Usage Notes

- QBR should be 80% about the customer's business and 20% about the product
- Never surprise the customer with bad news in a QBR -- address issues beforehand
- If the customer's executive sponsor cannot attend, consider rescheduling rather than proceeding without them
- Customize the value metrics section to reflect the customer's specific success criteria
- Include relevant case studies from similar customers to inspire next-quarter goals
