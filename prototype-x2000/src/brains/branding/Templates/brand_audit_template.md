# Brand Audit — Template

## Purpose

A brand audit is a systematic examination of a brand's current position in the market relative to its competitors and its own strategic objectives. This template structures the audit process to produce actionable findings that inform brand strategy decisions. The audit answers three questions: Where is the brand today? How did it get here? Where should it go next?

## Usage Notes

- This template should be adapted to audit scope; not all sections required for every audit
- Quantitative data sources should be cited with methodology, sample size, and confidence intervals
- The audit should be conducted by someone with brand expertise, not self-assessed by the brand team
- Findings should be evidence-based, not opinion-based
- Reference `06_measurement/` modules for measurement methodology guidance
- Archive completed audits for longitudinal brand health tracking
