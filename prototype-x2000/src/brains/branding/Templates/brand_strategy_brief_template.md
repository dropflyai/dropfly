# Brand Strategy Brief — Template

## Purpose

This template structures the strategic foundation for any brand initiative. It captures the essential strategic inputs that inform all downstream brand work: identity design, messaging development, campaign creation, and experience design. A completed Brand Strategy Brief is the single most important document in brand development — it is the contract between strategy and execution.
