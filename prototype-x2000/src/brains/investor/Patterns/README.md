# Investor Brain — Patterns Library

## Purpose

Patterns encode repeatable workflows for investor relations, fundraising, and capital management operations. Each pattern captures the complete process from initiation through execution, including quality gates, stakeholder coordination, legal checkpoints, and common failure modes.

---

## Pattern Inventory

| Pattern | File | Use Case |
|---------|------|----------|
| Fundraising Round | `fundraising_round_pattern.md` | Executing an equity fundraising round (Seed through Growth) |
| Board Management | `board_management_pattern.md` | Managing board of directors relationship and governance |
| Exit Planning | `exit_planning_pattern.md` | Planning and executing company exit (M&A or IPO) |

---

## When to Use Patterns

- **Fundraising Round Pattern:** When preparing for, executing, or closing any equity financing
- **Board Management Pattern:** Ongoing governance and board relationship management
- **Exit Planning Pattern:** When evaluating or pursuing M&A or IPO

---

## Cross-Brain Dependencies

| Pattern | Brain Dependencies |
|---------|-------------------|
| Fundraising Round | MBA Brain (business strategy), Finance Brain (financial modeling), Legal Brain (term sheets) |
| Board Management | MBA Brain (strategic planning), Finance Brain (reporting) |
| Exit Planning | Legal Brain (M&A/IPO), Finance Brain (valuation), MBA Brain (strategy) |
