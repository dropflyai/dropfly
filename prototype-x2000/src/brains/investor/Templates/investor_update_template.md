# Investor Update — Template

## Purpose

This template structures monthly or quarterly investor updates. Consistent, transparent investor communication builds trust, maintains engagement, and increases the likelihood that investors will provide help when asked. Investors consistently rank "quality of communication" as the top predictor of whether they will invest in a founder's next company. Conversely, radio silence between fundraising rounds is the fastest way to damage the investor relationship.

## Formatting and Distribution Notes

- **Frequency:** Monthly is best; quarterly is minimum. Monthly updates build stronger investor relationships
- **Length:** Maximum 1-2 pages in email body. Do not attach documents unless necessary
- **Format:** Plain text email or simple HTML. No PDFs, no slide decks, no attachments
- **Tone:** Conversational, honest, CEO's authentic voice
- **Send day:** Same day each month (e.g., first Tuesday) for predictability
- **Distribution:** BCC all investors (or use investor update tool: Carta, Visible, Foundersuite)
- **Archive:** Save every update; they become institutional memory and fundraising preparation material
