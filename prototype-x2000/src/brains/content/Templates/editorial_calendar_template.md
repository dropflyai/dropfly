# Editorial Calendar Template — Strategic Content Planning and Production Tracking

## How to Use This Template

This template structures the editorial calendar as a multi-layered
planning document. Layer 1 provides the annual strategic view. Layer 2
provides the monthly production plan. Layer 3 provides the weekly
execution tracker. Use all three layers together for complete editorial
operations visibility.

---

## Calendar Governance

### Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|---------|
| Editorial standup | Weekly | 15 min | Content team | Status, blockers |
| Editorial planning | Weekly | 30 min | Content + stakeholders | Next week planning |
| Monthly review | Monthly | 60 min | Content + marketing lead | Performance review |
| Quarterly strategy | Quarterly | 120 min | Content + leadership | Strategy alignment |

### Calendar Change Management

Changes to the published editorial calendar require:
- **Priority swap (P2↔P2):** Content lead approval
- **Content addition:** Content lead approval + capacity confirmation
- **Content removal:** Content lead + stakeholder who requested it
- **Theme change:** Director/VP approval
- **Emergency content:** Content lead approval, displaces lowest priority

---

**The editorial calendar is the operating system of content production.
It transforms strategy into scheduled execution and ensures nothing
falls through the cracks.**
