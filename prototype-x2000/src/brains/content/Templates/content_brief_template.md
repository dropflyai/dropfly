# Content Brief Template — Production-Ready Brief for Any Content Piece

## How to Use This Template

This template is the starting document for every content assignment.
Complete all required sections before assigning to a writer. Optional
sections may be omitted for simple content pieces but should be
completed for high-priority or complex content. The brief serves as the
contract between strategist and writer—if it is not in the brief, you
cannot hold the writer accountable for its absence.

## Brief Completion Checklist

Before assigning this brief to a writer, confirm:

[ ] Business objective is specific and measurable
[ ] Target audience is clearly defined
[ ] Unique angle is articulated (not a me-too brief)
[ ] Outline is detailed enough to guide writing
[ ] SEO requirements are researched and documented
[ ] Competitor content has been analyzed
[ ] Internal linking targets are identified
[ ] CTA is defined and URL is confirmed
[ ] Timeline is realistic given content complexity
[ ] All reference materials are accessible to the writer
[ ] Distribution plan is outlined

---

**A complete brief is the single most important investment in content
quality. The time spent here saves multiples in revision cycles later.**
