# Mutual Action Plan Template — Shared Roadmap from Evaluation to Go-Live

## Overview

### What Is This Document?

This Mutual Action Plan (MAP) is a shared document between [Your Company] and
[Buyer Company] that outlines every step both organizations must complete to move
from evaluation to successful deployment. It is a collaboration tool — not a
seller's closing plan. Both parties own it, both parties update it, and both
parties are accountable to the timeline.

### Why Use a Mutual Action Plan?

Research from Gartner and Winning by Design shows that deals with mutual action
plans close 25-40% faster and at 15-20% higher win rates than deals without them.
The reason is simple: a MAP creates mutual accountability, surfaces hidden blockers
early, and gives both parties visibility into the path forward.
