# QBR Template — Quarterly Business Review for Strategic Accounts

## Purpose

The Quarterly Business Review (QBR) is a structured strategic conversation between
your organization and a key customer. It is NOT a product demo, a support ticket
review, or a sales pitch. A QBR serves three purposes:

1. **Demonstrate value delivered** — Quantify the impact your solution has created
2. **Align on future strategy** — Understand evolving customer priorities and plan
3. **Strengthen the relationship** — Build executive-level rapport and trust

QBRs are the primary mechanism for driving expansion, preventing churn, and
building the executive relationships that make your product strategic rather than
tactical.

## QBR Agenda (60-90 Minutes)

### Opening (5 Minutes)

**Agenda Setting:**
"Thank you for making time for our quarterly review. Today's agenda:

1. Review the value we have delivered together this quarter
2. Discuss your evolving priorities for the coming quarter
3. Explore opportunities to deepen our partnership
4. Align on action items and next steps

Before we begin, has anything significant changed since we last spoke that
should shape today's conversation?"

### Section 3: Customer Strategic Priorities (15-20 Minutes)

**Objective:** Understand evolving customer needs. THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT
SECTION. Stop presenting and start listening.

**Questions to Drive Discussion:**

"Looking ahead to the next quarter (and beyond), what are your team's top 3
priorities?"

"How is your organization's strategy evolving? Any major initiatives launching?"

"What challenges are emerging that you did not anticipate?"

"How is your team growing or changing? New hires, reorganizations?"

"What does success look like for you personally in the next 6-12 months?"

---

**A great QBR leaves the customer feeling that the partnership is valuable, their
investment is justified, and the future together is exciting. A poor QBR feels like
a vendor update they tolerate. The difference is preparation, data, and genuine
curiosity about their world.**
