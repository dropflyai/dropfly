# Partner Review Template — Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

## Instructions

This template structures the Quarterly Business Review between your organization and a partner. Complete all sections with actual data before the QBR. Distribute the completed document as a pre-read at least 5 business days before the meeting. Use this document as the meeting agenda and record action items during the session.
