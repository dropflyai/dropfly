# Partnership Agreement Template

## Instructions

This template provides the structural framework for a partnership agreement. It is NOT a substitute for legal counsel. All agreements must be reviewed by legal before execution. This template ensures that all critical partnership terms are addressed during drafting. Customize sections based on partnership type and scope.

---

## PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Between:**
- [Company A full legal name] ("Company A"), a [state/country] [entity type], with its principal office at [address]
- [Company B full legal name] ("Company B"), a [state/country] [entity type], with its principal office at [address]

**Effective Date:** [Date]

**Agreement Number:** [Internal reference number]

**2.4 Limitations.** Nothing in this Agreement [grants either party the right to bind the other / creates an agency, employment, or joint venture relationship / restricts either party from engaging in its ordinary business activities].

## ARTICLE 3: COMMERCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Revenue Model.** [Select and customize:]

*Option A: Referral Fee*
Company [A/B] shall pay Company [B/A] a referral fee equal to [X%] of the first-year contract value for each Customer referred under this Agreement. Referral fees are due within [30/60] days of Customer payment.

*Option B: Revenue Share*
Company [A/B] shall pay Company [B/A] a revenue share equal to [X%] of recurring revenue for each Customer attributed to the partnership. Revenue share payments are due [monthly/quarterly] within [30] days of period close.

Company [B/A] shall purchase Company [A/B]'s product at [X%] discount from list price for resale to Customers.

**3.3 Payment Terms.** All payments are due within [30] days of invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at [X%] per annum.

**3.4 Minimum Commitments.** [If applicable: Each party commits to a minimum of $[X] in joint activities / [X] qualified referrals per quarter / etc.]

**3.5 Taxes.** Each Party is responsible for its own taxes arising from this Agreement.

## ARTICLE 4: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**4.1 Background IP.** Each Party retains full ownership of all intellectual property that existed prior to this Agreement ("Background IP"). Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of Background IP.

**4.2 Foreground IP.** Intellectual property created during and as a result of this partnership ("Foreground IP") shall be allocated as follows:
- [Option A: Creator owns, with license to the other party]
- [Option B: Joint ownership with mutual license]
- [Option C: Allocated by field of use]
- [Specify: What are the terms of any license granted?]

**4.3 Brand Usage.** Each Party grants the other a limited, non-exclusive, revocable license to use its trademarks, logos, and trade names solely in connection with the activities described in this Agreement, subject to the brand guidelines provided by the owning Party.

**4.4 Post-Termination IP.** Upon termination, [each Party retains its Background IP / Foreground IP license continues / Foreground IP license terminates within [X] days].

---

## ARTICLE 5: DATA PROTECTION

**5.1 Data Sharing.** The Parties agree to share the following data in connection with this partnership: [Specify data types, direction of sharing, and purpose].

**5.2 Data Processing.** All shared data shall be processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including [GDPR / CCPA / other applicable regulations].

**5.3 Data Security.** Each Party shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect shared data, including [encryption in transit and at rest / access controls / audit logging].

**5.4 Data Breach Notification.** Each Party shall notify the other within [24/48/72] hours of discovering a data breach affecting shared data.

**5.5 Data Deletion.** Upon termination of this Agreement, each Party shall delete shared data within [30/60/90] days, unless retention is required by law.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CONFIDENTIALITY

**6.1 Obligations.** Each Party agrees to protect the other's Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care.

**6.2 Permitted Disclosures.** Confidential Information may be disclosed to employees and contractors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

**6.3 Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party; (b) was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the receiving party; or (d) is received from a third party without restriction.

**6.4 Duration.** Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of [2/3/5] years.

---

## ARTICLE 7: GOVERNANCE

**7.1 Executive Sponsors.** Each Party shall designate an executive sponsor for this partnership:
- Company A: [Name, Title]
- Company B: [Name, Title]

**7.2 Partnership Managers.** Day-to-day partnership operations shall be managed by:
- Company A: [Name, Title]
- Company B: [Name, Title]

**7.3 Joint Steering Committee.** The Parties shall establish a Joint Steering Committee composed of [the executive sponsors and partnership managers / other designated individuals] that shall meet [quarterly] to review partnership performance and strategic direction.

**7.4 Decision Rights.** [Specify which decisions are made jointly, which are made unilaterally by each party, and the escalation path for disagreements.]

**7.5 Reporting.** Each Party shall provide the other with [monthly/quarterly] reports on partnership activity, including [pipeline, revenue, customer satisfaction, and other agreed metrics].

---

## ARTICLE 8: TERM AND TERMINATION

**8.1 Initial Term.** This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of [1/2/3] year(s).

**8.2 Renewal.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive [1]-year periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least [60/90] days prior to the end of the then-current term.

**8.3 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within [30] days of written notice; (b) becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy; or (c) [other specific termination triggers].

**8.4 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement without cause upon [90] days' written notice.

**8.5 Effect of Termination.**
- All outstanding payments shall become due within [30] days
- Each Party shall return or destroy the other's Confidential Information
- Customer transition plan shall be executed within [60] days
- [Specify any surviving obligations — IP licenses, non-compete, non-solicitation]

**8.6 Wind-Down Period.** Following termination, the Parties shall cooperate for a period of [90] days to transition any shared customers, unwind integrations, and complete pending transactions.

---

## ARTICLE 9: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**9.1** Each Party represents and warrants that: (a) it has the authority to enter into this Agreement; (b) its performance will not violate any other agreement; (c) it will comply with all applicable laws.

**9.2** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS AGREEMENT, NEITHER PARTY MAKES ANY WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

---

## ARTICLE 10: LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**10.1** EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY, DATA PROTECTION, OR IP PROVISIONS, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

**10.2** EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY, DATA PROTECTION, OR IP PROVISIONS, EACH PARTY'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED [the greater of $[X] or the total fees paid in the [12] months preceding the claim].

---

## ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

**11.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [State/Country].

**11.2 Dispute Resolution.** [Mediation, then binding arbitration / Exclusive jurisdiction of courts in [location]]

**11.3 Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party, except in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.

**11.4 Force Majeure.** Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to causes beyond its reasonable control.

**11.5 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with its Schedules, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter hereof.

**11.6 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both Parties.

**11.7 Notices.** All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above.
