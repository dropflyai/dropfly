# Partnership Proposal Template

## Instructions

This template is used to propose a new partnership to a prospective partner organization. Complete all sections with specific, evidence-based content. Remove instructional text before sending. The proposal should be 3-5 pages and demonstrate that you have done your homework on the prospective partner.
