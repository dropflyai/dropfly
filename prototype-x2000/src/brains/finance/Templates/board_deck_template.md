# Board Deck Template — Quarterly Board Meeting Presentation

## Instructions

This template structures the quarterly board meeting presentation. Customize
the content for each meeting but maintain the consistent structure so board
members know where to find information. Send the completed deck plus supporting
materials at least 5 business days before the board meeting. Limit the main
deck to 15-20 slides; place supplementary detail in the appendix.

  2. Analysis: data and frameworks applied

## Pre-Meeting Checklist

- [ ] All financial data finalized and reconciled
- [ ] Deck reviewed by CFO and CEO
- [ ] Strategic topics vetted with key board members (no surprises)
- [ ] Appendix materials prepared
- [ ] Deck sent to board 5+ business days before meeting
- [ ] Admin: calendar invite, video link, room booked
- [ ] Board minutes template ready
- [ ] Prior meeting action items tracked and updated
