## Section 8: Experiment Quality Checklist

Post-experiment self-assessment:
- [ ] Hypothesis was specific and falsifiable
- [ ] Success/failure criteria were defined before the experiment ran
- [ ] We did not move the goalposts during the experiment
- [ ] Sample size was sufficient for confidence in results
- [ ] We tested one variable at a time (or controlled for multiple)
- [ ] Results were analyzed objectively (not confirmation bias)
- [ ] Decision was made based on data, not politics
- [ ] Learnings were documented for future reference
