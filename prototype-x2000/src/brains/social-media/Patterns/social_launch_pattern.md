# Pattern: Social Media Launch Campaign

## Pattern ID

`SOCIAL-LAUNCH-001`

## Classification

Campaign execution pattern for product launches, feature releases, brand launches, and major campaign activations on social media.

---

## When to Use This Pattern

- Launching a new product or product line
- Releasing a major feature or version update
- Launching a brand or sub-brand on social media
- Activating a major marketing campaign (seasonal, promotional, awareness)
- Re-launching or repositioning an existing brand/product

---

## Prerequisites

Before initiating this pattern, ensure:

- [ ] Product/feature is confirmed for launch date
- [ ] Brand guidelines and messaging framework are finalized
- [ ] Target audience personas are documented (see 02_strategy/audience_strategy.md)
- [ ] Platform strategy is defined (Tier 1, 2, 3 platforms identified)
- [ ] Budget is allocated (content production, paid media, influencer)
- [ ] Team capacity is confirmed for launch period
- [ ] Legal/compliance review process is available

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch Strategy (T-6 to T-4 Weeks)

### 1.1 Launch Strategy Document

| Deliverable | Content |
|-------------|---------|
| Launch objectives | SMART goals (awareness target, engagement target, conversion target) |
| Audience mapping | Which personas, on which platforms, at which funnel stage |
| Messaging framework | Key messages, value propositions, proof points, tone |
| Platform strategy | Platform-specific approach for each Tier 1 and 2 platform |
| Content plan | Content types, volume, timeline per platform |
| Paid media plan | Budget, targeting, creative approach, objectives |
| Influencer plan | Creator tiers, selection criteria, deliverables, timeline |
| Measurement plan | KPIs, tracking infrastructure, reporting cadence |

### 1.2 Tracking Infrastructure

| Setup Item | Tool | Deadline |
|-----------|------|----------|
| Campaign UTM parameters | UTM builder | T-6 weeks |
| Platform pixels verified | Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag | T-5 weeks |
| Custom conversion events | Platform ad managers | T-5 weeks |
| GA4 campaign tracking | Google Analytics | T-5 weeks |
| Unique tracking links per channel | Bitly, UTM | T-4 weeks |
| Social listening queries | Brandwatch/Sprout | T-4 weeks |
| Reporting dashboard | Looker Studio/Sprout | T-4 weeks |

### 1.3 Influencer Identification and Outreach

| Action | Timeline | Details |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Identify 20-50 potential creators | T-6 weeks | Using selection criteria from 06_influencer/influencer_strategy.md |
| Verify audience authenticity | T-5 weeks | HypeAuditor or similar tool |
| Initial outreach | T-5 weeks | Personalized outreach per outreach framework |
| Negotiate and contract | T-4 weeks | Deliverables, timeline, compensation, usage rights |
| Ship product samples | T-4 weeks | Ensure creators have product before content creation begins |

## Phase 4: Launch Execution (T-Day)

### 4.1 Launch Day Timeline

| Time | Action | Channel | Owner |
|------|--------|---------|-------|
| 6:00 AM | Verify all scheduled posts are queued | All platforms | Social coordinator |
| 7:00 AM | Email blast to subscriber list | Email | Email marketing |
| 8:00 AM | Hero launch post goes live | Instagram, LinkedIn | Scheduled |
| 8:15 AM | Support post with key features/benefits | X, Threads | Scheduled |
| 8:30 AM | TikTok launch video published | TikTok | Scheduled |
| 9:00 AM | Paid campaigns activated | Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok | Paid social manager |
| 9:00 AM | Employee advocacy posts go live | LinkedIn (employee accounts) | Employee advocates |
| 9:00-12:00 | Active community management | All platforms | Community team (all hands) |
| 10:00 AM | Influencer posts begin publishing | Per influencer schedule | Influencer manager monitoring |
| 12:00 PM | Story series with product deep-dive | Instagram Stories | Scheduled |
| 12:00-1:00 PM | Performance check #1 | Analytics dashboard | Social media manager |
| 2:00 PM | Engagement boost post (user question/poll) | All platforms | Social coordinator |
| 3:00 PM | Second wave of influencer content | Per schedule | Influencer manager |
| 5:00 PM | Performance check #2 | Analytics dashboard | Social media manager |
| 5:00 PM | Evening content (behind-the-scenes) | Instagram Stories, TikTok | Social coordinator |
| 8:00 PM | Day 1 performance summary | Internal report | Social media manager |
| Ongoing | Comment/DM response (<1 hour response time) | All platforms | Community team |

### 4.2 Real-Time Optimization

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Top-performing organic content identified | Boost with paid amplification immediately |
| Low-performing content identified | Adjust caption, hashtags, or replace in schedule |
| Engagement spike on specific creator content | Request additional content from that creator |
| Negative comments/feedback emerging | Activate response protocol per 08_crisis guidelines |
| Technical issues (broken links, wrong images) | Fix immediately, repost if necessary |

## Phase 5: Post-Launch Momentum (T+1 to T+14 Days)

### 5.1 Sustained Content Calendar

| Day | Content Focus | Platforms |
|-----|--------------|-----------|
| T+1 | Customer reactions and social proof | Instagram, TikTok, X |
| T+2 | Deep-dive feature content | LinkedIn, Instagram carousel |
| T+3 | UGC roundup (first customer content) | Instagram, TikTok |
| T+4 | FAQ / "You asked, we answered" | Stories, Threads |
| T+5 | Use case / Tutorial content | All platforms |
| T+7 | One-week results celebration | All platforms |
| T+10 | Customer testimonial spotlight | Instagram, LinkedIn |
| T+14 | Launch retrospective / Thank you | All platforms |

### 5.2 Paid Media Optimization

| Optimization Action | Trigger | Timing |
|-------------------|---------|--------|
| Reallocate budget to top performers | Creative with >2x average ROAS | T+3 |
| Pause underperformers | Creative with <50% of average performance | T+3 |
| Expand lookalike audiences | Sufficient conversion data (50+ events) | T+5 |
| Launch retargeting campaigns | Audience pools from launch engagement | T+3 |
| Scale winning campaigns | Proven ROAS above threshold | T+7 |
| Creative refresh | Original creative reaching frequency cap | T+10 |

## Phase 6: Analysis and Reporting (T+14 to T+21)

### 6.1 Launch Performance Report

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Executive summary | 3-5 sentence overview of launch performance vs. goals |
| KPI dashboard | Actual vs. target for each launch KPI |
| Platform breakdown | Per-platform performance analysis |
| Content analysis | Top and bottom performing content with analysis |
| Paid media analysis | ROAS, CPA, spend, reach, conversions by campaign |
| Influencer analysis | Per-creator performance, EMV, engagement, conversions |
| Audience insights | New followers acquired, demographic shifts, engagement patterns |
| Competitive impact | Share of voice change, competitor response |
| Learnings | Key insights for future launches |
| Recommendations | Specific actions for sustained momentum and next launch |

### 6.2 Pattern Update

After completing the launch, document:
- What worked better than expected (amplify in future launches)
- What underperformed (diagnose and adjust for next time)
- Timeline adjustments needed (were deadlines realistic?)
- Resource gaps identified (what additional capacity is needed?)
- Tool or process improvements (what should be added/changed?)

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Measurement | Target Setting |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Total launch impressions | Sum across all platforms | >X impressions in first 14 days |
| Engagement rate | Weighted average across platforms | >X% above average |
| Website traffic from social | GA4 social channel report | >X sessions in launch period |
| Conversions attributed to social | Attribution model + UTM tracking | >X conversions / $X revenue |
| Influencer content engagement | EMV + direct engagement metrics | >X EMV from influencer program |
| New followers gained | Net new across all platforms | >X net new followers |
| Sentiment ratio | Positive:Negative from social listening | >8:1 positive:negative |
| Share of voice | Brand SOV during launch period | >X% increase from baseline |

## Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Prevention |
|-------------|------|------------|
| Launching without teaser buildup | No anticipation, flat launch engagement | Minimum 2-week teaser campaign |
| All content on one day, nothing after | Spike then silence | 14-day post-launch content calendar |
| No paid amplification | Limited reach, missed opportunity | Budget allocated for launch amplification |
| Influencer content not coordinated | Scattered, unfocused launch narrative | Coordinated posting schedule and briefs |
| No real-time monitoring on launch day | Missed engagement opportunities and potential issues | All-hands monitoring, hourly performance checks |
| Skipping post-launch analysis | No learning captured, same mistakes repeated | Mandatory launch retrospective within 3 weeks |

---

*This pattern should be adapted to your specific context. Timelines are guidelines; adjust based on product complexity, team size, and market conditions. The principle remains: plan thoroughly, execute precisely, learn systematically.*
