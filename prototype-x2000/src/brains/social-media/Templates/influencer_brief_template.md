## Disclosure and Compliance

### FTC Disclosure Requirements (MANDATORY)

All content must include clear and conspicuous disclosure of the paid partnership:

| Platform | Required Disclosure |
|----------|-------------------|
| Instagram | Use "Paid partnership" tag AND include "#ad" in first line of caption |
| TikTok | Activate "Branded content" toggle AND verbal mention in video |
| YouTube | Activate "Includes paid promotion" AND verbal disclosure in first 30 seconds |
| LinkedIn | Include "Paid partnership" or "#sponsored" prominently in post |
| All platforms | Disclosure must be visible without clicking "more" or scrolling |

**Non-compliance with FTC disclosure requirements may result in campaign termination and jeopardize future partnership opportunities. The brand may also face legal consequences.**

### Content Claims Restrictions

| You CAN Say | You CANNOT Say |
|-------------|---------------|
| "I love this product" (personal opinion) | "[Product] cures/treats [condition]" (medical claim) |
| "This has been helpful for me" (personal experience) | "Everyone will see results" (universal claim) |
| "I've noticed [specific result]" (personal observation) | "[Product] is clinically proven" (without documentation) |
| [Additional permitted claim] | [Additional prohibited claim] |
