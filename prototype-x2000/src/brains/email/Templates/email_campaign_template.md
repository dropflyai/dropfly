**Subject line checklist:**
- [ ] No spam trigger words (FREE, URGENT, ACT NOW)
- [ ] Personalization tested (merge tag renders correctly)
- [ ] Mobile preview checked (truncation at 35-40 chars)
- [ ] Clearly communicates value or action
- [ ] Aligns with email body content (no bait-and-switch)

## Section 4: Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Unsubscribe link present and functional
- [ ] Physical mailing address in footer
- [ ] From and subject line accurate (not misleading)
- [ ] Recipients have opted in to receive this type of email
- [ ] Consent records available for all recipients
- [ ] Suppression list applied (unsubscribes, bounces, complaints)
- [ ] Content compliant with relevant regulations (FTC, GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL)
- [ ] Legal review completed (if claims, pricing, or promotional offers)
