# Growth Model Template — Quantitative Forecasting for Revenue and User Growth

## How to Use This Template

This template structures a complete growth model that forecasts users,
revenue, and unit economics. Fill in the assumptions section with your
best estimates, then calculate the projections. Update monthly as actual
data replaces estimates. Build three scenarios (bear, base, bull) for
strategic planning.
