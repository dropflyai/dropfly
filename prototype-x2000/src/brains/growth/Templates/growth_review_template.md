# Growth Review Template — Monthly Growth Team Performance Review

## How to Use This Template

Complete this template monthly for the Growth Team's performance review.
Present to growth team and stakeholders. The review covers metrics
performance, experiment results, learnings, and next-month priorities.
Maintain a consistent format month-over-month for trend analysis.
