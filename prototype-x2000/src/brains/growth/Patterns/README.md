# Growth Brain — Patterns Library

## Purpose

This directory contains reusable growth patterns—proven, repeatable
frameworks for common growth challenges. Patterns encode the growth
team's institutional knowledge, ensuring that successful strategies
are replicated and failed approaches are documented for avoidance.

---

## Available Patterns

### viral_loop_pattern.md
**When to use:** Designing or optimizing a viral growth mechanism
(referral program, inherent virality, or content-driven sharing).
**Covers:** Loop design, K-factor optimization, cycle time reduction,
incentive structure, measurement framework.

### activation_optimization_pattern.md
**When to use:** Improving the conversion rate from sign-up to
activated user (first value delivery).
**Covers:** Activation event identification, onboarding funnel
optimization, time-to-value reduction, experiment playbook.

### retention_rescue_pattern.md
**When to use:** Addressing declining retention metrics or responding
to a churn spike.
**Covers:** Diagnosis framework, cohort analysis, intervention design,
re-engagement sequences, measurement and iteration.

---

## Pattern Usage Protocol

1. Check patterns before starting any growth initiative
2. Follow the pattern structure; deviate only with documented reasoning
3. Update patterns when new evidence improves the approach
4. New patterns require 3+ successful applications before formalization
5. Log all pattern applications and outcomes in Memory/

---

**Patterns make our best growth work repeatable and our institutional
knowledge permanent.**
