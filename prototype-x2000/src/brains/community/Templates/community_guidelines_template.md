## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- **Be respectful and kind.** Treat others the way you want to be treated.
  Disagree with ideas, not people.

- **Be helpful.** If you can help someone, do. Share your knowledge
  generously. Everyone was a beginner once.

- **Be constructive.** Feedback should be specific, actionable, and
  delivered with good intent. "This does not work" is not helpful.
  "This might work better if..." is.

- **Be inclusive.** Welcome newcomers. Use language that includes rather
  than excludes. Respect the diversity of our community.

- **Be on topic.** Post content in the appropriate channels. Keep
  discussions relevant to the community purpose.

- **Be patient.** Not everyone can respond immediately. Give people time
  to think and reply thoughtfully.

- **Give credit.** When sharing others' work, provide attribution. When
  building on others' ideas, acknowledge them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in any community space:

### Harassment and Discrimination
- Personal attacks, insults, or name-calling
- Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, gender identity,
  sexual orientation, age, disability, religion, or any protected
  characteristic
- Unwelcome sexual attention, advances, or content
- Threats of violence or intimidation
- Deliberate misgendering or use of rejected names

### Harmful Content
- Hate speech or promotion of hatred against any group
- Content glorifying violence, self-harm, or substance abuse
- Illegal content or instructions for illegal activities
- Malware, phishing links, or social engineering attempts
- Doxxing (sharing others' personal information without consent)

### Community Disruption
- Spam, excessive self-promotion, or solicitation
- Posting identical content across multiple channels
- Deliberately derailing or hijacking conversations
- Trolling or engaging in bad faith
- Impersonating other members, staff, or public figures
- Creating multiple accounts to evade enforcement

### Professional Standards
- [Industry/community-specific standards, e.g.:]
- Sharing confidential or NDA-protected information
- Disparaging competitors or their communities
- Providing dangerous advice (legal, medical, financial) without
  appropriate disclaimers

## Content Guidelines

### On-Topic Content

Each channel/space has a specific purpose. Please post content in the
appropriate location:

| Channel/Space | Purpose | What Belongs | What Does Not |
|--------------|---------|-------------|---------------|
| [#general] | [Community-wide discussion] | [Broad community topics] | [Support questions] |
| [#help] | [Getting help] | [Questions, troubleshooting] | [Unrelated chat] |
| [#showcase] | [Sharing work] | [Projects, demos, portfolios] | [Self-promotion] |
| [#off-topic] | [Casual conversation] | [Non-community topics] | [Anything violating guidelines] |

### Self-Promotion Policy

Self-promotion is welcome when it:
- Provides genuine value to community members
- Is posted in appropriate channels
- Includes context about why it is relevant
- Is not the member's only form of participation

Self-promotion is not welcome when it:
- Is posted in channels not designated for it
- Adds no value beyond advertising
- Is repeated or spammed
- Comes from someone who never participates otherwise

### Content Formatting

- Use descriptive titles for posts and threads
- Format code with code blocks
- Use appropriate content warnings for sensitive topics
- Keep messages concise and readable
- Search before posting to avoid duplicates

## Enforcement

### How We Handle Violations

When a guideline is violated, our moderation team will respond based on
the severity:

| Level | Response | Examples |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Gentle redirect** | Private message with guidance | Off-topic post, minor formatting |
| **Warning** | Private written warning | First-time minor violation |
| **Temporary mute** | Restricted posting (1–24 hours) | Repeated minor violations |
| **Suspension** | Account restricted (7–30 days) | Serious violations, harassment |
| **Permanent ban** | Account permanently removed | Severe violations, threats |

### Enforcement Principles

- **Private first:** We address issues privately before taking public action
- **Progressive:** Severity increases with repeat violations
- **Consistent:** Same rules apply to everyone, regardless of tenure or status
- **Documented:** All enforcement actions are documented
- **Appealing:** You may appeal any enforcement action (see below)
