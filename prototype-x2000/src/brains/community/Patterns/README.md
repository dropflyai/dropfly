# Community Brain — Patterns

Patterns are reusable, proven approaches to common community challenges.
Each pattern documents a specific scenario, the forces at play, the
solution structure, and implementation guidance.

---

## Available Patterns

| Pattern | File | Use When |
|---------|------|----------|
| Community Launch | `community_launch_pattern.md` | Launching a new community from scratch |
| Engagement Revival | `engagement_revival_pattern.md` | Reviving a declining or stagnant community |
| Platform Migration | `platform_migration_pattern.md` | Moving a community to a new platform |

---

## How to Use Patterns

1. **Identify** the scenario you face
2. **Read** the relevant pattern completely
3. **Adapt** the pattern to your specific context
4. **Execute** following the pattern's phases
5. **Document** deviations and learnings for future reference

---

## Pattern Structure

Each pattern follows this format:

- **Context:** When and why this pattern applies
- **Forces:** The tensions and constraints in play
- **Solution:** The recommended approach
- **Implementation:** Step-by-step execution guide
- **Metrics:** How to measure success
- **Anti-Patterns:** Common mistakes to avoid
- **Examples:** Real-world applications

---

**Patterns are not rigid templates. They are distilled experience that
accelerates decision-making. Adapt them to your context, but understand
the reasoning before deviating.**
