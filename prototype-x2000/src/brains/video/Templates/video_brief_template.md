# Video Brief — Template

## Purpose

This template captures all strategic, creative, and logistical inputs required to produce a video. A completed brief is mandatory before any production begins. The brief serves as the contract between the requester and the production team — it defines success, prevents scope creep, and ensures every video is aligned to a measurable business objective.
