### Positioning Implications

| Quadrant | Our Strategy |
|----------|-------------|
| We are in Premium | Maintain if brand supports it; ensure perceived value justifies premium |
| We are in Best Value | Defend this position; this is the strongest competitive position |
| We are in Budget | Either raise price with value add, or own the cost-leadership position |
| We are Overpriced | Urgent: either increase value delivery or reduce price |

## Section 8: Data Collection Methods

### How to Gather Competitive Pricing Data

| Method | Quality | Effort | Notes |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| Competitor pricing pages | High (public) | Low | Screenshot and archive monthly |
| G2/Capterra/TrustRadius reviews | Medium | Low | Filter for pricing mentions |
| Sales team intelligence | High | Medium | Structured debrief after competitive deals |
| Customer interviews | High | High | Ask about alternatives considered and pricing |
| Mystery shopping | High | Medium | Sign up for competitor trials |
| Industry analyst reports | High | Medium | Gartner, Forrester pricing sections |
| Job postings | Low-Medium | Low | Budget signals from competitor hiring |
| SEC filings (public companies) | High | Medium | ARPU, revenue per customer data |
| Third-party data providers | Medium-High | High | Services like PriceIntelligently, Klue |
