# Pricing Brain -- Patterns Index

> Reusable pricing patterns for recurring monetization scenarios. Each pattern provides strategic frameworks, execution steps, measurement criteria, and anti-patterns for common pricing challenges.

---

## Available Patterns

| Pattern | File | When to Use |
|---------|------|----------|
| Price Change Execution | `price_change_pattern.md` | Implementing a price increase, decrease, or restructuring |
| Good-Better-Best Packaging | `packaging_pattern.md` | Designing tiered pricing packages for SaaS or subscription products |
| Freemium to Paid Conversion | `freemium_pattern.md` | Optimizing the conversion funnel from free users to paying customers |

---

## How to Use Patterns

1. Identify the pricing challenge you are facing.
2. Select the matching pattern from the table above.
3. Read the full pattern before beginning execution.
4. Gather the required data inputs (usage data, willingness-to-pay, competitive pricing).
5. Adapt the execution steps to your market context, product maturity, and competitive position.
6. Measure outcomes against the metrics defined in the pattern.
7. Document deviations and learnings in `Memory/README.md`.

---

## Pattern Quality Standards

Every pricing pattern must:
- Ground recommendations in established pricing theory (Van Westendorp, Conjoint, value-based pricing).
- Include quantitative decision criteria (not just qualitative judgment).
- Address customer communication and change management.
- Include anti-patterns from common pricing mistakes.
- Define measurable success criteria with specific metrics.
