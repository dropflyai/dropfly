# Legal Memorandum Template

> A structured template for documenting legal research, analysis, and recommendations on specific legal questions. Follows the IRAC methodology (Issue, Rule, Application, Conclusion).

> **DISCLAIMER:** This template is for educational and informational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice. The analysis contained in any memorandum prepared using this template should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before reliance.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, verify:
- [ ] All issues are addressed with analysis and conclusion
- [ ] Applicable law is current and correctly cited
- [ ] Both sides of each argument are considered
- [ ] Assumptions are clearly stated
- [ ] Risk classification is assigned
- [ ] Recommendations are actionable
- [ ] Disclaimer is included
- [ ] Privileged/confidential marking is appropriate
- [ ] Reviewed by at least one additional attorney (for HIGH/CRITICAL)
