# Research Brain — Patterns

Reusable research patterns for common strategic scenarios. Each pattern provides a proven sequence of methods, activities, and deliverables.

---

## Available Patterns

| Pattern | Description | Duration | Use When |
|---------|-------------|----------|----------|
| Market Entry Research | Full research program for evaluating a new market | 8-12 weeks | Entering a new market or launching a new product category |
| User Research Sprint | Rapid, focused user research within a design sprint timeframe | 1-2 weeks | Validating user needs or testing concepts quickly |
| Competitive Analysis | Comprehensive competitive landscape assessment | 4-6 weeks | Strategic planning, market positioning, or competitive response |

---

## How to Use Patterns

1. Select the pattern that matches your scenario.
2. Adapt the template to your specific context (adjust methods, timelines, and scope).
3. Use the pattern as a starting framework, not a rigid prescription.
4. Document deviations from the pattern and the reasons for them.
5. After completing the pattern, contribute improvements back.

---

## Pattern Quality Standards

- Every pattern must have been successfully executed at least once.
- Patterns include estimated timelines, resource requirements, and deliverables.
- Patterns reference specific methods from the research brain modules.
- Patterns are reviewed annually for continued relevance.

---

**Patterns are living documents. Update them as better approaches are discovered.**
