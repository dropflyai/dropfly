# Template: Support Playbook

## Purpose

This template provides the structure for creating a comprehensive support playbook that
defines how a support organization operates. A playbook is the single source of truth
for all support processes, standards, and procedures. Every support team should have
one. Fill in all bracketed sections with organization-specific details.

---

## How to Use This Template

1. Copy this template to a new document
2. Replace all `[bracketed text]` with your organization's specifics
3. Remove any sections that do not apply to your current stage
4. Review with the support leadership team for completeness
5. Distribute to all support staff and adjacent teams
6. Review and update quarterly
