### Report Purpose

This template provides a structured framework for marketing performance reporting.
It is designed to communicate results at the appropriate level of detail for each
audience: executives need the headline metrics and strategic implications; the
marketing team needs channel-level diagnostics and optimization recommendations.

**Reporting Principle:** Every metric shown must answer one of three questions:
1. Are we on track to hit our targets?
2. If not, why?
3. What are we doing about it?
