# Industry Intelligence Framework — Authoritative

You can't design for a market you don't understand.
Industry intelligence grounds design in business reality.

---

## Purpose

Industry intelligence exists to:
- understand market dynamics and trends
- identify key players and their positioning
- discover regulatory and compliance requirements
- anticipate technology shifts
- inform product positioning

Design without industry context is guesswork.

## Industry-Specific Research Guides

### B2B SaaS
```
FOCUS AREAS:
- Integration ecosystem
- Enterprise vs SMB split
- Security/compliance requirements
- Pricing model trends
- Customer success expectations

KEY SOURCES:
- G2 Grid reports
- Gartner Magic Quadrant
- Forrester Wave
- SaaS industry reports
```

### FinTech
```
FOCUS AREAS:
- Regulatory landscape (varies by region)
- Security certifications needed
- Banking partnerships
- Consumer trust factors
- Open banking trends

KEY SOURCES:
- Financial regulatory bodies
- Banking industry publications
- Fintech startup trackers
- Consumer financial surveys
```

### Healthcare / HealthTech
```
FOCUS AREAS:
- HIPAA/health data regulations
- Provider vs patient focus
- EHR integration requirements
- Telehealth trends
- Clinical validation needs

KEY SOURCES:
- Healthcare industry journals
- CMS/regulatory bodies
- Health IT publications
- Patient advocacy research
```

### E-commerce / Retail
```
FOCUS AREAS:
- Consumer behavior shifts
- Mobile commerce trends
- Payment preferences
- Fulfillment expectations
- Personalization standards

KEY SOURCES:
- eMarketer
- Shopify/BigCommerce reports
- Consumer surveys
- Retail industry publications
```

### Developer Tools
```
FOCUS AREAS:
- Developer experience expectations
- Open source landscape
- Integration requirements
- Documentation standards
- Community expectations

KEY SOURCES:
- Stack Overflow surveys
- GitHub trends
- Developer communities
- Tech blog sentiment
```

## Synthesis Questions

After gathering data, answer these:

```
1. What are the 3 biggest trends shaping this industry?


2. What regulatory/compliance factors must we address?


3. Who are we really competing against (including non-obvious)?


4. What do buyers actually prioritize when choosing?


5. What technology integrations are expected/required?


6. What's the biggest unmet need in the market?


7. How should this inform our visual/UX approach?


```
