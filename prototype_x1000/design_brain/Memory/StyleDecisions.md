### Color Learnings

| Industry | Works | Doesn't Work | Why |
|----------|-------|--------------|-----|
| FinTech | Blues, Greens | Bright reds (except alerts) | Trust, stability |
| Healthcare | Blues, Soft greens, White | Dark, heavy colors | Calm, clinical |
| E-commerce | Depends on brand | Muted conversion CTAs | Energy drives action |
| B2B SaaS | Professional blues, neutrals | Overly playful colors | Credibility |
| Consumer | Brand-dependent | Off-brand colors | Personality match |

## Layout Decisions

### Density Learnings

| Audience | Preferred Density | Why |
|----------|-------------------|-----|
| Beginners | Low (lots of whitespace) | Reduced overwhelm |
| Power users | Medium-High | Efficiency valued |
| Executives | Low-Medium | Quick scanning |
| Developers | High | Information density valued |

## Imagery & Illustration Decisions

### Illustration Style Learnings

| Industry | Works | Doesn't Work |
|----------|-------|--------------|
| Tech/SaaS | Abstract, geometric | Clip art, stock photos |
| Healthcare | Warm, human-centered | Cold, clinical (unless clinical product) |
| Finance | Data visualization, charts | Playful illustrations |
| Consumer | On-brand personality | Generic stock |

## Animation & Motion Decisions

### Motion Learnings

| Context | Works | Doesn't Work |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| Onboarding | Subtle guidance animations | Overwhelming motion |
| Data-heavy apps | Minimal, purposeful | Decorative animations |
| Consumer apps | Delightful micro-interactions | Slow transitions |
| Enterprise | Professional, restrained | Playful bounces |
