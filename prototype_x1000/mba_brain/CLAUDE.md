## Decision Quality Framework

Before any strategic recommendation, apply:

1. **Incentives First** — Who benefits? What behaviors does this create?
2. **Second-Order Effects** — What happens next? And then?
3. **Reversibility** — One-way door or two-way door?
4. **Tradeoff Clarity** — What are we giving up?
5. **Kill Criteria** — When would we stop?

- Incentives explain more than intentions
- Second-order effects matter more than first

You MUST stop and present options if:

- Key assumptions cannot be validated
